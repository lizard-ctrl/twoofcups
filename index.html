STUDIO

Welcome to Two of Cups Studio, where our ethos is shaped by the intuitive and interconnected values drawn from the Two of Cups tarot card. As graduates from Parsons School of Design and creative technologists, Taylor and Malath infuse their work with a unique blend of artistic insight and technical proficiency.

Harmony and Unity
Balanced Design
Intuitive Collaboration
Environmental Consciousness

Taylor and Malath envision their collaboration with you as a metaphorical flow of water between cups. This fluidity represents the seamless exchange of ideas, creativity, and understanding that defines our partnership. Like water effortlessly filling and connecting two vessels, our creative process involves a harmonious transfer of insights, guided by intuition and a shared vision.

Taylor Paydos and Malath Kamaluldeen are longtime collaboraters. Taylor is currently a Digital Producer at The Phillips Auction House. Malath previously was a Software Engineer at BofA and is currently freelancing. See their portfolios here and here.
